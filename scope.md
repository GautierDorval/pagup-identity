# Scope

Pagup is a web-focused entity operating at the intersection of:

- advanced SEO and indexing architecture,
- semantic structuring of web content,
- AI and LLM interpretability governance.

Its activities are centered on technical architecture, not on generic digital marketing
or mass content production.

---

## What Pagup does

- Designs and maintains technical tooling related to crawl control, indexing, and semantic clarity.
- Deploys governance layers that define how AI systems and crawlers should interpret web content.
- Works on real-world WordPress-based ecosystems where ambiguity, volatility, or scale require strict control.

---

## What Pagup does not do

- Pagup is not a generalist web agency.
- Pagup does not provide turnkey marketing campaigns.
- Pagup does not operate as a content production studio.
- Pagup does not sell universal or packaged SEO solutions.

Its work is intentionally specialized and architecture-driven.
